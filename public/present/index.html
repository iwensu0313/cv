Seaweed Aquaculture in Southern California, Santa Barbara, March 2018.

Presenter, “Testing the Economic Feasibility of Seaweed Aquaculture in Southern California,” Master Project Defenses, Bren School, Santa Barbara, March 2018. Presented project scope and results to an academic panel. Presentations were open to the public. PRESENTATION OVERVIEW: Globally, aquaculture systems produce 23.8 million tonnes of seaweed, worth $6.4 billion USD annually. The majority of edible seaweeds consumed in California is imported from Asia and any local seaweed product is overwhelmingly sourced from wild harvesters in Northern California.
